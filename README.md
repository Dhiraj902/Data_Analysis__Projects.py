# Motorola Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
Project Objective
To build an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Motorola mobile sales data and present key insights on sales performance, regional trends, customer preferences, and product performance. The objective is to support better business decisions through clear, visual, and data-driven analysis.

## DATASET
<a href = "https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/waqi786/mobile-sales-dataset">Dataset</a>

I have used this dataset to analyze Motorola mobile sales performance, understand customer behavior, identify top-performing brands and models, track regional trends, and study payment methods and customer ratings through an interactive Power BI dashboard.


##KPI Questions

What is the total sales revenue generated?

How many units (quantity) have been sold in total?

How many transactions were completed?

What is the average sales value per transaction?

Which brand is performing best in terms of sales?

Which mobile model has the highest sales?

Which city/region contributes the most to total sales?

What is the monthly sales trend? (growth/decline)

Which payment method is used most by customers?

How do customer ratings impact sales performance?

On which day of the week are the most units sold?

What is the top 5 products by sales amount?

ðŸ”„ Project Process

Data Collection
Collected the mobile sales dataset in Excel format containing transaction-level details such as date, city, brand, model, quantity, sales, payment method, and customer ratings.

Data Cleaning & Preparation
Cleaned the data by removing duplicates, handling missing values, correcting data types, and standardizing column names using Power BI / Power Query.

Data Modeling
Created relationships between tables (if applicable) and prepared the data model for analysis.

KPI Definition
Defined key performance indicators such as Total Sales, Total Quantity, Total Transactions, and Average Sales.

DAX Measures Creation
Wrote DAX measures to calculate KPIs and other metrics used in visuals.

Dashboard Design
Designed an interactive and user-friendly layout with cards, charts, tables, maps, and slicers.

Visualization & Analysis
Built visuals for monthly trends, city-wise performance, brand/model comparison, payment methods, and customer ratings.

Interactivity
Added slicers and filters for Month, Brand, Model, Payment Method, and Ratings to allow dynamic analysis.

Insights & Validation
Analyzed results, validated KPIs, and checked consistency with raw data.

Publishing & Documentation
Saved the report, exported screenshots, and documented the project on GitHub with description, objectives, dataset, KPIs, and process.
